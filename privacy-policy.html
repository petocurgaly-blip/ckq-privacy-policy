Privacy Policy – Chess Knight's Quest

Last updated: July 2025

Chess Knight's Quest is a free puzzle game developed as an independent project. This page explains our privacy policy.

Data Collection

Chess Knight's Quest does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. The app does not require registration or login.

Local Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device only:

  • Your best scores for each starting square
  • Your preferred language setting
  • Your display name (only if you choose to enter one for score sharing)

This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to the developer or any third party.

Third-Party Services

The app does not use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Children

Chess Knight's Quest is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect any data from children or adults.

Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, you can reach us via the contact information provided on the Google Play Store listing.
